### Follow-up Work (Phase 02.2 — inserted 2026-05-16)

Phase 02.1 closed the worst of the hydration/perceived-load gap. User reported residual mobile-Safari sluggishness ("buttons unresponsive 1-3 s") that Phase 02.1 alone didn't address. Phase 02.2 (commits `0a73069` through `81284d4`) shipped the additional fixes:

- Conditional Paddle SDK loading (only on `/pay`) — removed a 500-1500 ms render-blocking script from 8 of 9 routes
- Vendor chunk split (vendor-react / vendor-router / vendor-lucide) — main bundle 328 → 107 KB
- Self-hosted WOFF2 fonts with preload — eliminated Google Fonts 3-RTT cascade on Safari
- `<link rel=preload as=script>` Safari fallback for `modulepreload` (Safari < iOS 17 ignores modulepreload)
- `touch-action: manipulation` to kill the 300 ms tap delay on legacy Safari builds
- Lazy-loaded EN i18n dict — saves 40 KB raw / 13 KB gzip on every RU visit
- Mobile-resolution `srcset` for feature cards + steps image (PageSpeed-flagged 93 KB savings)
- `font-display: swap` for both webfonts with adjusted-fallback metric handling

Phase 02.2 is tracked inline in commit history (no `.planning/phases/02.2-*/` directory created since the work was reactive rather than pre-planned). All commits landed on `main` via direct push and are live on production.
