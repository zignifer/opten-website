- **404 catch-all design** — Minimal `NotFound` component: locale-aware short message, link to home, `<meta name="robots" content="noindex">` injected via prerender's existing meta pipeline. Add `<Route path="*" element={<NotFound/>}/>` to `src/main.tsx`. No fancy "did you mean?" search.

Schema-level `inLanguage` stays region-specific (`ru-RU`, `en-US`).
