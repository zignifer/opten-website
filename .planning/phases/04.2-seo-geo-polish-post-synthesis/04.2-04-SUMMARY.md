## Impact

**Accessibility:**
- Screen-reader users on the welcome page now hear 4 distinct, step-numbered descriptions of the onboarding flow instead of 4× "Opten extension". Restores the educational value of the carousel for non-sighted users.

**Performance:**
- First-paint PNG fetches drop from 4 → 1 (only slide 1 is `loading="eager"`; the browser defers slides 2-4 until they rotate into view via the 3 s `setInterval`). Saves ~3 PNG decodes on the network/LCP critical path.

**AI crawlers / SEO:**
- Welcome page now carries 4 differentiated alt strings describing the onboarding flow, rather than 4 identical placeholders. Differential signal for crawlers; closes A-7 from synthesis.
