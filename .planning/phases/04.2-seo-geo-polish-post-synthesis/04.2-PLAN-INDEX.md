## Dependency notes

- **Wave 4 ↔ Wave 6 file overlap (WelcomePage.tsx):** Wave 6 depends on Wave
  4 because both touch `src/app/pages/WelcomePage.tsx`. Wave 4 ships the
  carousel alt/lazy hygiene FIRST (no markup-structure change), Wave 6 adds
  the `<h1>` above the carousel on top of Wave 4's diff. This keeps the
  approval-gated commit small and isolated to the h1 addition.

  but per Phase 4.1's pattern + the morning batch `260517-t5i` convention,
  the executor runs them sequentially (one atomic commit per wave) for
  clean rollback boundaries.
