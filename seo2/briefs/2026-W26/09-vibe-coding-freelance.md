## Article Angle

Do not present vibe coding as magic. The real value is task framing, product thinking, design judgment, testing and deployment. This article should bridge strongly to learning and course CTA.

## Practical Case

Prompt "сделай сайт для кофейни" gives a generic page. A better brief includes audience, offer, sections, visual style, CTA, constraints, mobile requirements and content. That turns AI coding into a draft a human can evaluate.
