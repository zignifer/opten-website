## Article Angle

Good commercial use-case: small business wants sales materials, not AI art. Teach a practical workflow and naturally bridge to learning/Opten.

## Outline Suggestions

1. Что нужно продавцу на маркетплейсе.
2. Where AI helps: photo, background, copy, infographics, ads.
3. Prompt for product visual.
4. Prompt for description.
5. Quality/legal checks.
6. How this can become a service.

## Practical Case

Для товара нужен новый визуал и описание. Vague prompt creates a generic stock-like image. A prompt with material, angle, scale, buyer pain, benefits and marketplace constraints produces a more commercial draft.
