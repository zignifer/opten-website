This is a tool-selection article, but it should not become a giant directory. Use a decision framework: task, input, output, review, prompt quality, cost/credits, privacy.

## Outline Suggestions

## Practical Case

Reader asks "which AI should I learn?" Better answer: choose one task first. For example, presentation workflow needs outline, slide copy and visual direction; the prompt matters more than switching tools.
