## Suggested Placement

- Title/H1: exact `бесплатное обучение нейросетям` or natural close variant.
- Intro: clarify what free learning can realistically give: first workflows, vocabulary, small projects.
- H2 ideas: `Что можно выучить бесплатно`, `Что не заменит бесплатный курс`, `Как собрать первый кейс`.
- FAQ: `Есть ли бесплатные курсы по ИИ?`, `Можно ли начать с нуля бесплатно?`, `Когда нужен платный курс?`

## Article Angle

Treat "free" carefully: good for acquisition, weaker for conversion. The article should build trust by not overselling. Free lessons are enough to test the direction and make first outputs; deeper progress needs practice, feedback and consistency.

## Outline Suggestions

1. Где бесплатное обучение действительно работает.
2. Какие темы учить первыми: prompts, text, presentations, visuals, video.
3. Как не попасть в "смотрю уроки, но ничего не делаю".
4. 5 практических заданий на неделю.
5. Как Opten помогает делать задания лучше.

## Practical Case

Пользователь смотрит три ролика подряд и не получает навыка. Альтернатива: после каждого урока сделать один маленький результат, проверить prompt через Opten, сохранить до/после и собрать мини-портфолио.

## Visual Production Brief

Pattern: follow the W23 visual pattern and the current SEO2 image rules. This is
part of the article brief and is a blocker for image generation. Do not start
from generic image hints or a textless base.

### Hard Rules

- Cover: one shared RU/EN raster at `public/blog/free-ai-courses/cover.jpg`, 1600x900,
  16:9, no in-image text, no logos.
- Inline images: 4+ RU rasters and 4+ EN rasters. Short text must be generated
  inside the final image, not added afterward with editor, Canvas, HTML/CSS,
  Sharp, or any other overlay/post-processing step.
- Typography for all visible generated text: Bebas Neue only. Attach/use
  `seo2/Reference/bebas-neue-font-reference.png` as the font reference.
- Brand color: exact Opten lime `#9CFB51` on deep teal-black `#011417`.
- Batch ban: do not reuse dark desk + floating UI board + laptop dashboard +
  lime connector network motifs. Do not reuse composition, camera angle, or
  object layout from another article in this batch.

### Cover Concept

- Scene family: hands-on learning setup with lesson cards, task sheets, practice checklist, portfolio result, and one strong physical anchor object.
- Concept: free AI learning roadmap, no text.
- Composition: one strong physical or cinematic subject, editorial tech mood,
  useful crop-safe negative space, no text.
- Avoid: fake app UI, readable cover words, generic laptop dashboard, floating
  connector network, course-hype stock imagery, repeated W26 motif.

### Inline Frames

1. RU `ЗАДАЧА` / EN `TASK`: watch less, build more. Make it a concrete visual explanation of `бесплатное обучение нейросетям`, not a decorative slide.
2. RU `ПРАКТИКА` / EN `PRACTICE`: first five tasks. Use physical cards, marked objects, cinematic preview frames, or real workflow artifacts instead of a bare UI grid.
3. RU `PROMPT` / EN `PROMPT`: Как не попасть в "смотрю уроки, но ничего не делаю". Show the prompt/brief layer as part of the scene, with the label rendered by the image generator.
4. RU `КЕЙС` / EN `CASE`: 5 практических заданий на неделю. Show the checked final result or decision point; keep text short and legible.

### Information Layer

Every inline frame must follow the strong W23 reference pattern from
`seo2/Reference/ai-headshot-step-*.jpg` and `seo2/Reference/ai-ugc-step-*.jpg`:
one large hero label plus 3-4 generated-in-image supporting labels or
micro-cards. Use icons, checklist rows, score chips, timeline nodes,
before/after markers, flow arrows, anatomy callouts, or decision-tree cards.

For each RU/EN frame, define before generation:

- hero label from the Inline Frames list;
- 3-4 supporting labels, each short enough to render cleanly;
- one visual object/icon for each supporting label;
- one visible structure: checklist, scorecard, timeline, before/after, matrix,
  flow, anatomy, or decision tree.

Title-only images are rejected. Decorative icons without readable explanatory
labels are rejected. The frame must teach one concrete decision from the article
even if viewed without the surrounding paragraph.

Inline typography: final generated raster, Bebas Neue only, exact `#9CFB51`,
large readable label. Attach/use `seo2/Reference/bebas-neue-font-reference.png`.
No text overlays after generation.
